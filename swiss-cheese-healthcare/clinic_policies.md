# Swiss Cheese Healthcare Clinic — Patient Guide

## Booking Appointments

New and returning patients can book appointments in three ways:

1. **Patient Portal** — Log in at portal.swisscheeseclinic.com to view availability and self-schedule.
2. **Phone** — Call our front desk at (555) 234-8900, Monday through Friday, 7:30am to 6:00pm.
3. **Walk-in** — Walk-in hours are Monday through Friday, 8:00am to 11:00am. Wait times vary.

Follow-up appointments are typically scheduled at checkout by our front desk staff. If you need to reschedule, call or use the patient portal at least 24 hours in advance.

## Insurance & Billing

We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Copays are collected at check-in. For billing questions, contact our billing department at billing@swisscheeseclinic.com or (555) 234-8901.

## Prescription Refills

Refill requests should be submitted through the patient portal or by calling the pharmacy line at (555) 234-8902. Please allow 48 hours for processing. Controlled substances require an office visit for refill authorization.

## Cancellation Policy

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Missed appointments without notice may incur a $50 no-show fee. Late arrivals of 15+ minutes may need to be rescheduled.

## After-Hours & Emergencies

For life-threatening emergencies, call 911. For urgent after-hours concerns, call our nurse line at (555) 234-8999. A triage nurse is available 24/7 to advise whether you should visit the ER, urgent care, or wait for a regular appointment.
